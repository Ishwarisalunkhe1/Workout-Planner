Monday – Full Body Starter

Jumping Jacks

Jump feet out and arms overhead, then back in, repeatedly.

Bodyweight Squats

Sit back like a chair, knees over toes, then stand tall.

High Knees

Run in place, lifting knees to waist height quickly.

Wall PushUps

Stand arm’s length from wall, bend elbows to bring chest close, then push back.

Glute Bridges

Lie on back, lift hips up squeezing glutes, then lower.

Forward Lunges

Step forward, bend both knees to 90°, push back to start.

Plank Hold

Hold body straight on forearms and toes, core tight.

Mountain Climbers

From plank, drive knees toward chest alternately, fast.
