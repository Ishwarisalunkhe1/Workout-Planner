Tuesday – Core & Neck Posture

Plank Hold

Keep your body straight, elbows under shoulders, core tight.

Glute Bridge Hold

Lie on back, lift hips, squeeze glutes, hold.

Bird Dog

On all fours, extend opposite arm & leg, switch sides.

Side Plank

Lie on side, lift hips, body in a straight line.

Chin Tucks

Gently pull chin straight back, hold, then release.

Shoulder Blade Squeeze

Pull shoulder blades together, keep shoulders relaxed.

Jumping Jacks

Jump feet out and arms overhead, then back in, repeatedly.

Superman Hold

Lie on belly, lift arms, chest, and legs, hold.
