Cool-Down Exercise

Forward Bend

Steps:

  1. Stand up tall with feet hip-width apart.
  2. Inhale deeply, then exhale slowly as you bend forward from your hips.
  3. Let your arms hang down toward your toes (you can slightly bend your knees if needed).
  4. Keep your head and neck relaxed, hanging down naturally.
  5. Feel the stretch in your back, hamstrings, and calves.

Benefits:

  • Relieves tension in the spine and legs, improves flexibility, and calms the nervous system.
