Cool-Down Exercise

Child Pose

Steps:

  1. From your hands and knees, slowly sit your hips back toward your heels.
  2. Stretch your arms forward and rest your forehead on the mat or floor.
  3. Relax your shoulders and breathe deeply.

Benefits:

  • Gently stretches the lower back, hips, and thighs. Promotes relaxation and calms your mind.
