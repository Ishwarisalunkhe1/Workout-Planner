Warm-Up Exercise

Hip Circles

Steps

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Place hands on your hips.
  3. Slowly rotate your hips in a circular motion â€“ forward, side, back, side (like drawing a big circle).
  4. Do this in one direction for 30 seconds, then switch directions.

Purpose

  • Helps warm up hip joints and lower back.
  • Improves flexibility and balance.
  • Prevents injury during squats and lunges.

Tips

  • Keep knees slightly bent.
  • Focus on moving hips only, not the entire body.
