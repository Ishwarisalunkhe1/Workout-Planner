Warm-Up Exercise

March In Place

Steps:

  1. Stand tall with feet hip-width apart.
  2. Begin lifting one knee up toward your chest, then lower it.
  3. Alternate legs, like walking in one spot.
  4. Swing your arms naturally as if walking.
  5. Keep your back straight and core slightly tight.

Purpose:

  • Gently increases heart rate.
  • Warms up hips, thighs, and arms.
  • Gets your body moving without impact.
