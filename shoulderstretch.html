Cool-Down Exercise

Shoulder Stretch

Steps:

  1. Stand or sit comfortably.
  2. Bring your right arm across your chest.
  3. Use your left hand to gently press your right arm closer to your body.
  4. Keep the stretched arm straight, not bent.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. 30 seconds on right arm, then 30 seconds on left arm. Total: 1 minute

Benefits:

  • Relieves shoulder tightness, improves range of motion, and relaxes the upper body.
