Cool-Down Exercise

Cat-Cow Pose

Steps:

  1. Get on your hands and knees (tabletop position).
  2. Cat Pose: Exhale, round your back, tuck your chin to your chest, and pull your belly toward your spine.
  3. Cow Pose: Inhale, arch your back, lift your head and tailbone toward the ceiling.

Benefits:

  • Increases spinal flexibility, reduces stiffness, and improves posture.
