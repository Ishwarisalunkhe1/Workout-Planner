Warm-Up Exercise

Ankle Circles

Steps:

  1. Stand near a wall or chair if needed for balance.
  2. Lift one foot slightly off the ground.
  3. Rotate the ankle in slow, full circles.
  4. Do this for 30 seconds, then switch to the other foot.
  5. You can do this seated if standing is hard.

Purpose

  • Warms up ankle joints.
  • Reduces risk of twisting ankles during jumping/cardio.
  • Increases mobility before exercise.

Tips

  • Make full, slow circles—don’t rush.
  • Point your toes gently as you rotate.
