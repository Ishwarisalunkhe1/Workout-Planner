Main Workout Roadmap

Monday – Full Body Starter

Begin the week with a complete body warm-up and foundational exercises.

Tuesday – Core & Neck Posture

Focus on abdominal strength and improving neck alignment.

Wednesday – Leg, Balance & Neck

Enhance lower body strength and stability with posture correction.

Thursday – Upper Body Strength & Neck Alignment

Build upper body endurance while maintaining proper posture.

Friday – Cardio & Core with Neck Stretch

Boost stamina with cardio and finish with essential stretches.

Saturday – Active Recovery Strength

Low-intensity strength training to recover and maintain mobility.

Sunday – Rest & Recovery

A day dedicated to full recovery and relaxation.
